Friendship Circle - 6th Annual Walk With Friendship

I was responsible for overseeing the Friendship Circle's Annual 5k, as well as creating all the design collateral for the event, which included one 8.5" x 11" and one 11" x 17" poster, promotional postcard, lawn sign, and a variety of small items for the walk itself

Cornish College of the Arts - 2019 Giving Week Fundraising Campaign

Every year Cornish puts on a week-long fundraising campaign to raise money for student scholarships. I was tasked with creating all design collateral to include digital and print material. I created the primary logo for the campaign, incorporated on all materials associated with the campaign.

Cornish College of the Arts - 2019 Calendar

The 2019 calendar was the first big marketing piece for the Advancement Department of Cornish College. I came up with the concept and design based on the new brand Cornish planned to role out in January 2019.

Cornish College of the Arts - 2019 Neddy Awards Catalog

Each year Cornish hosts the Neddy Awards, which showcases artwork for six artists in multiple formats. Shortly after I was hired, I was tasked with creating a catalog for the event.  Below is a 52-page spread with artist work and bios as well as the history of the award.

Friendship Circle - 2018 Heroes Gala

The Friendship Circle of Washington hosts a gala every Spring to raise money for their organization.  I was responsible for creating the design collateral, to include this 112-page Gala Journal, for the event.  I also assisted in planning and overseeing the event with its co-chairs.
