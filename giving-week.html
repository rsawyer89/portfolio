Cornish College of the Arts - 2019 Giving Week Fundraising Campaign

Every year Cornish puts on a week-long fundraising campaign to raise money for student scholarships. I was tasked with creating all design collateral to include digital and print material. I created the primary logo for the campaign, incorporated on all materials associated with the campaign.
